5 Tips for a Smooth Office Move

October 24, 2025 â€¢ by Sarah Jenkins

Moving an entire office is a monumental task that requires careful planning and execution. Here are our top 5 tips to ensure everything goes smoothly.

1. Start Early

Begin planning at least 3-6 months in advance. This gives you enough time to handle logistics, notify employees, and coordinate with movers.

2. Assign a Project Manager

Designate one person or a small team to oversee the entire process. This ensures clear communication and accountability.

3. Purge and Declutter

Use the move as an opportunity to get rid of old files, broken furniture, and outdated equipment. Don't pay to move things you don't need.

"The secret to a successful move is detailed planning and open communication with your team."

4. IT Infrastructure First

Make sure your IT team has a plan to get servers and internet up and running at the new location before everyone else arrives.

5. Communicate with Employees

Keep your team informed about the timeline, what they need to pack, and what to expect at the new office.
